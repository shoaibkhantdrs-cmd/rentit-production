# RentIt User Manual (v1.0)

## Creating an account

Sign up with your name, email, and (optionally) phone number. You'll be sent a verification code by email and/or SMS — enter it to verify. You can log in afterward with a password, or without one via a one-time code sent to your email/phone.

## Searching for a rental

Use the search page to filter by category, price, bedrooms/bathrooms, and location — results can be sorted by distance if you share your location. Save a search to get notified when new matching listings appear (Saved Searches, under your account menu).

## Viewing a listing

Each listing shows photos, price, features, and the owner's basic info. Favorite it to save for later, or message the owner directly (chat or WhatsApp, depending on what the owner has enabled) to ask questions or arrange a viewing.

## Listing your own property

From "List a property," fill in the details, add photos (up to 10 per listing), and submit. New listings go through a brief moderation review before appearing in search — you'll be notified either way, and if rejected, you'll see the reason and can edit and resubmit.

**Boosting a listing**: from "My properties," boost a listing or upgrade to a premium plan to get better placement in search results. Payment is handled via Razorpay or Stripe, whichever you choose at checkout — your card/UPI details never touch RentIt's own servers directly.

## Messaging

Conversations appear under "Messages," updating in real time while you're online. You can also reach an owner over WhatsApp directly from a listing page, if they've made that available.

## Identity verification

Some actions (contacting owners, listing a property — depending on how the platform is configured) may require verifying your identity once. Submit the requested document from the Verification page; you'll be notified once it's reviewed.

## Notifications

Control what you're notified about, and through which channel (email, SMS, push, in-app), from Notification Preferences. Push notifications require allowing them when prompted by your browser or the mobile app.

## Payment history

Every payment, invoice, and refund is available under "Payment history" — download an invoice any time.

## Using RentIt on your phone

RentIt works as an installable app directly from your mobile browser — look for an "Add to Home Screen" (iOS Safari) or "Install app" (Android Chrome) prompt, or add it manually from your browser's menu. Installed this way, it works offline for pages you've already visited and opens without browser address-bar clutter. Native Android/iOS app store versions are in preparation (see the release notes for current status).

## Getting help

If something looks wrong or you need to report a listing or user, use the Report option available from the relevant page — it goes directly to RentIt's moderation team.
